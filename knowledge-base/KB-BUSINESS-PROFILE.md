# Business Profile

## What should I put in the Business Name field?

Put the exact trading name your callers know you by. This is what Q says when it answers: "Good morning, Paul's Plumbing." If your business is known by a shortened name or trading name rather than your registered company name, use that. Q sounds more natural saying "Bloom & Co" than "Bloom and Company Limited."

The business name also appears in messages sent to callers after a call, on your booking page, and in any notifications. Keep it as callers would expect to see it.

## What should I put in the Opening Hours field?

Write your opening hours in plain English exactly as you would say them to a caller. Q reads this directly and tells callers when you are available.

**Example:** Monday to Friday 8am to 6pm. Saturdays 9am to 1pm. Closed Sundays and bank holidays.

You do not need to use a specific format. Q is intelligent enough to understand natural language. However, be specific — "weekdays" is fine, "most days" is not. If your hours vary by season or you have different hours for different services, write that in.

If you are always available, write: Available around the clock — Q handles all calls.

## What is the Business Context field for?

Business Context is free text that tells Q the background story of your business — what makes you different, how long you have been trading, where you are based, what kind of jobs you specialise in, anything a knowledgeable receptionist would know.

Q uses this to answer questions like "how long have you been going?" or "do you cover my area?" or "what kind of work do you specialise in?"

**Example:** Based in Wigan, covering Greater Manchester and Lancashire. Trading for 12 years. Specialists in gas boiler installation, servicing, and emergency breakdown. All engineers are Gas Safe registered. Typical jobs completed same or next day.

Be generous here. The more Q knows about your business the more confident and accurate it sounds. Aim for three to five sentences. You can always add more.

## What is the Contact Name field?

This is your first name, or the name of the person Q should refer callers to. Q uses it in conversations and in messages sent after calls.

**Example of Q using it:** "I'll make sure Mark knows you called and he'll get back to you shortly."

**Example in a message:** "Hi Sarah, thanks for calling. Mark will be in touch shortly."

If your business has a front-of-house person rather than the owner taking calls, use their name. If callers know you by your first name, use your first name. If you trade under a business name and it sounds odd to name an individual, use something like "the team" — but a personal name almost always sounds warmer.

## What is the Booking Link field?

This is the web address where callers can book online. If you use an online booking system — Calendly, Acuity, Fresha, your own website booking page — paste the full URL here.

Q will offer this link to callers who want to book, and it is included in after-call messages when the booking link message type is enabled.

If you do not use online booking, leave this blank. Q will offer a callback instead of a link.

**What to put:** The full URL starting with https://. Test it first to make sure it goes to the right page.

## What is the Business Email field?

This is the email address where Qerxel sends your notifications — new lead alerts, urgent escalation emails, weekly summaries. It is not shown to callers.

Use the email address you check regularly. If you want notifications to go to more than one person, set up a forwarding rule on your email. Qerxel sends to one address only.

## What is the Business Phone field?

This is your real phone number — the one Q calls when there is an urgent escalation and it needs to alert you by phone. It is also used to send you SMS notifications if you have those enabled.

It is not the number your customers call. That is your Qerxel number, which you set up call forwarding from.

## What does the Booking Link on the Business Profile do differently from the one in AI Behaviour?

They are the same link — set it in either place and it updates everywhere. The booking link in your business profile is the master version. It is used in Q's conversations, in after-call messages, and on your booking page. You only need to set it once.
