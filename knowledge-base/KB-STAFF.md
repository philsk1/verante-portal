# Staff and Team

## Who should I add to the Team?

Add anyone who works in your business whose name a caller might ask for — team members who take bookings, work specific services, or have their own regular clients.

Q uses your team list in two ways:
1. **During calls** — when a caller asks for a specific person, Q confirms who they are asking for and gives their direct line if one is listed
2. **Caller intelligence** — if a team member calls in from their listed phone number, Q recognises them as staff and handles the call as an internal call

If you run your business alone, adding yourself is still useful — it gives Q your name for personalised callbacks and greeting.

---

## What should I put in the Name field?

Use the name your clients and callers know them by — first name and surname if needed, or just first name if that is how they are known. If a caller says "Can I speak to Mark?" Q needs the name to match.

---

## What is the Role field?

Role is a free-text description of the team member's position — Owner, Senior Stylist, Engineer, Therapist, Manager, Receptionist. Q uses this when a caller asks who a person is: "Mark is our senior engineer."

Keep roles short and plain. Callers don't need job titles — they need enough to understand who can help them.

---

## What are Specialist Services?

Specialist services tell Q what each team member is best at. If a caller asks "who does your eyelash extensions?" Q can say "That would be Emma — she's our specialist for eyelash work."

Enter services exactly as they appear in your service catalogue, or close enough that Q can match them. Multiple services can be listed per team member.

---

## What is a Direct Line?

A direct line (DID) is a specific phone number a caller can ring to reach one team member directly. If a team member has a direct line, Q mentions it when a caller asks for them.

This is most useful for businesses where staff have their own client relationships — a personal trainer, a specialist therapist, a senior engineer with long-term clients.

Leave this blank if your team all share the same business number.

---

## What is the Colour field?

The colour is used only in the calendar view — it colour-codes this team member's appointments so you can quickly tell whose bookings are whose when viewing the calendar. Click the colour circle to pick from a palette.

This has no effect on how Q speaks or how the booking page looks.

---

## What is the team member's phone number used for?

The phone number you add to a team member's profile is used for caller intelligence — if someone calls your Qerxel number from this number, Q recognises them as a staff member and handles the call accordingly.

If your team members often call in to pass messages or check bookings, adding their personal mobile numbers here means Q will greet them by name and handle the call correctly rather than treating them as a customer.

---

## What is Staff Availability?

Staff availability tells the calendar when each team member is available to take bookings. If you have the multi-staff calendar tier enabled, the booking page shows customers which team members are available at which times.

For each team member you can set:
- Which days they work
- What hours they work on each day
- Whether their availability is active or paused

If a team member is on holiday or unavailable for a period, you can temporarily deactivate their availability rather than deleting and re-creating their schedule.

---

## How does the booking page show staff availability?

When a customer chooses a service on the booking page, the calendar shows available slots. If you have multiple staff, slots are generated from the team members who offer that service and whose availability covers the requested time.

Customers can optionally select a specific team member before choosing a time, or they can pick a time and see which team member is available. This depends on your booking page configuration.

---

## Can Q book appointments with specific team members?

If provisional booking is enabled (multi-staff plan required), Q can offer slots and specify which team member is available. Q checks the live calendar and only offers times that are genuinely free for the relevant staff member.

If provisional booking is not enabled, Q takes the caller's details and confirms someone will call back to arrange a time.

---

## What if a caller asks for a team member who is not available?

Q does not have access to real-time staff availability during calls unless provisional booking is active. If a caller asks specifically for a team member, Q confirms who they are and passes the message to the owner — it does not promise availability or tell the caller when that person is free.

If you want Q to know about specific unavailability, add it to your Additional Instructions in AI Behaviour: "Mark is unavailable on Mondays — do not promise same-day contact on Mondays."

---

## How many team members can I add?

There is no hard limit on the number of staff profiles you can create. However, if you have more than about eight team members, the calendar column view becomes crowded. The multi-staff calendar tier supports up to twenty columns.

---

## Can I temporarily remove a team member without deleting them?

Yes — set the team member's profile to inactive. Inactive staff still exist in the system but Q does not mention them in conversations and they do not appear in the booking page's staff selection. Their past appointments are preserved in the calendar.

To reactivate them, set the profile back to active.
