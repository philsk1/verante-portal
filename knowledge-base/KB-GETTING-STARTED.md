# Getting Started

## What is Qerxel and what does it do?

Qerxel is an AI call-handling system for small businesses and sole traders. When someone calls your business number and you are unable to answer, Qerxel's AI — Q — answers on your behalf. Q introduces itself as your AI assistant, handles the conversation professionally, takes details, offers your booking link, refers callers to the right person or partner, and logs everything for you to review.

Q is not a voicemail. It holds a real conversation, adapts to what the caller says, and knows your business — your services, your staff, your opening hours, your rules. Every call is recorded, summarised, and tagged with an outcome so you always know what happened and what needs your attention.

Nothing about your setup is fixed. You configure Q through the portal and it updates immediately on your next call.

## How do I get started after signing up?

After signing up, Qerxel automatically creates your AI assistant in the background. Your first job is to fill in your business details so Q knows who it represents. Work through the portal in this order:

1. **Business Profile** — Set your business name, opening hours, and your name as the contact. These are the minimum Q needs to answer calls properly.
2. **AI Behaviour** — Configure how Q handles different call types, what tone it uses, and what happens at the end of each call.
3. **Services** — Add what you offer. Q will describe your services to callers and use them to identify what each call is about.
4. **Staff** — If you have team members, add them so Q can direct callers to the right person.
5. **AI Settings → Save** — Every time you make changes, save them and Q updates within seconds.

Check the **Before you go live** checklist on your dashboard. It shows exactly which items are complete and which still need attention. When everything is green, you are ready.

## What is the Before You Go Live checklist?

The checklist on your dashboard shows seven items that must be in place before Q can handle real calls:

- **AI assistant created** — Done automatically when you sign up
- **Phone number assigned** — A Qerxel number needs to be linked to your account. Contact support to request one.
- **Business name set** — Q uses this in every greeting
- **Opening hours set** — Q tells callers when you are available
- **Contact name set** — Q mentions your name when offering a callback
- **AI instructions set** — Additional context that helps Q handle your specific calls
- **Services in catalogue** — Q needs to know what you offer

The checklist disappears automatically once all items are green. Until then it stays visible as a prompt.

## What does it mean when it says I need a phone number assigned?

For Q to answer real inbound calls, a phone number must be linked to your account. When a caller dials this number, the call goes to Q. You set up call forwarding on your existing business number so callers can still ring the number they know — it just reaches Q instead of your voicemail.

You do not need to change your public-facing phone number. You simply forward calls to your Qerxel number. The process takes about thirty seconds on any phone.

To request your Qerxel number, contact support. Once assigned, the checklist item goes green and Q is able to take live calls.

## How do I set up call forwarding?

Call forwarding sends calls from your existing business number to your Qerxel number. The exact steps depend on your phone provider, but the principle is the same everywhere.

**On most UK mobile networks:**
- Dial **\*\*61\*[your Qerxel number]#** to forward calls when unanswered (after a few rings)
- Dial **\*\*62\*[your Qerxel number]#** to forward calls when your phone is off or out of signal
- Dial **\*\*67\*[your Qerxel number]#** to forward calls when you are on another call

Most clients use the unanswered forward — Q picks up when you do not. You can also forward all calls if you want Q to handle everything.

To cancel forwarding, dial **##61#**, **##62#**, or **##67#** depending on which type you set.

If you have a landline or VoIP number, your provider's customer service can set this up for you in a few minutes. Tell them you want conditional call forwarding to a specific number.

## How quickly does Q update when I change my settings?

Changes you save in the portal take effect on your very next call — usually within a few seconds. Q does not need to be restarted. When you click Save on any settings page, your AI configuration updates in the background. The sync status chip next to the save button confirms when the update is complete.

If you are mid-call when you save changes, the current call continues with the old settings. The new settings apply from the next call onwards.

## Can I test Q before going live?

Yes. On the AI Behaviour page, scroll down to the **Test your AI** section. Enter your mobile number and click **Call me now**. Q will ring your phone within a few seconds and hold a real conversation using your actual configured AI — your services, your tone, your instructions.

You need to have saved your AI settings at least once before testing. Q uses your real configuration for the test call, not a generic demo. This ensures what you hear during testing is exactly what your callers will hear.

## What happens to calls before I have everything set up?

If a caller reaches Q before your setup is complete, Q will still answer and handle the conversation to the best of its ability using whatever information is available. It may not know your full service list or be able to mention specific staff members, but it will always take a message and log the call.

For this reason it is worth completing your setup before activating call forwarding. The readiness checklist on your dashboard tells you when you have enough in place to go live confidently.
